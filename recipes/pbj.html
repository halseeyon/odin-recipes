Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

"Hey, Hal, why do you have a PB&J recipe on your website? Can't anyone over the age of 4 make that by heart?" Yes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar peanut butter (whatever kind you like, although the correct answer is crunchy)
  • 1 jar jam (of your choice, but again, I must emphasize that grape jelly is the correct option)
  • OPTIONAL: a little bit of butter

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread and place them side-by-side.
  2. Coat one slice evenly with peanut butter.
  3. Coat the second slice with your jelly.
  4. Stick the two slices of bread together so the peanut butter and jelly are touching.
  5. OPTIONAL: coat the bottom of a non-stick pan with butter. Toast both sides of sandwich until they are golden brown.
