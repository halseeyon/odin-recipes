Baked Potato

Description

Don't be fooled. This isn't a loaded baked potato or anything. Just a plain ol' potato as the world intended.

Ingredients

  • one potato
  • OPTIONAL: some butter

Steps

  1. Before you start, wash the potato and poke a bunch of holes in it with a fork, at least 5 times but preferably more. I cannot emphasize this enough. If you do not do this step it will likely result in your potato exploding in the microwave. Seriously. Just do it.
  2. Place in the microwave (on a microwave-safe dish!) and cook for 5 minutes or however long is recommended by your microwave manufacturer.
  3. Flip the potato over and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Finish off by placing in a 425-degree oven until the skin on the outside of the potato is as crisp as you want.
  5. OPTIONAL: When finished and cool enough to eat, but still warm, cut the potato down the middle and add a slice of butter. Yum!
